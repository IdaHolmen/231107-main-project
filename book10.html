Fysikkmagi

Andreas Wahl

In "Fysikkmagi" Andreas Wahl shows you the magic of physics. You get to satisfy your curiosity when it comes to sky phenomena and everyday occurrences. You get challenged to try it yourself with little, cool experiments. It's about wondering and exploring the world we have around us. Andreas will show you how!
