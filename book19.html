Anatomi for småtasser

Jonathan Litton

Anatomy for Babies is a baby book with information about the senses, organs and other body parts. Your child will learn how to point to their brain, heart and lungs as they follow along with the colourful illustrations. The flip-flap mechanism and baby characters will appeal to young children, making this book a wonderful way of captivating your little one's attention while teaching them about their body.

Genre: Non-Fiction

Age group: Kids

Publication year: 2018
