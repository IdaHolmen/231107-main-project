Trines kokebok for unge kokker

Trine Sandberg

Children should be allowed to experiment in the kitchen and make food they like, says Norways biggest and most respected food blogger, Trine Sandberg. With "Trines kokebok for unge kokker" she wants to inspire the young ones in the family to spend more time cooking for themselves and their family.

Genre: Non-fiction

Age group: Kids, Young adult

Publication year: 2021
