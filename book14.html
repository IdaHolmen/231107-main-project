Tungtvannsaksjonen

John S. Jamtli

Hardangervidda, 1942. Agent Einar Skinnarland is dropped in a parachute over Hardangervidda. His mission is to rapport about everything that's happening at the heavy water facility in the basement of Norsk Hydro's power station at Vemork, to find out how to sabotage it. Only his superior in England, Leif Tronstad, knows how important the mission will be - that heavy water could be detrimental to Germanys attempt to produce a bomb that could give them a upper hand in the war.
