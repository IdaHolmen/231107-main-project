De uverdige

Roy Jacobsen

Roy Jacobsen's novel "De Uverdige" follows a gang of boys and girls from an apartment building on the east side of Oslo during the german occupation of Norway. They are living in poverty, but contributes to their families finances in creative ways by conning, stealing, forging and planning elaborate break-in's. They aren't scared to take advantage of their enemies either.

Genre: Fiction

Age group: Adult
