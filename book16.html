Farvel, Farah Diba

Karin Fossum

In Fossum's new crime novel a man comes rushing in to the police station, limping and screaming that his 6 year old daughter has gone missing. An unknown girl has been observed in the storage of a Joker store and in a blue van by a gas station, near the forested area of Natvig. Has Kandis been abducted by a predator? Has the little girl in the beatiful dress been brought far into the dark woods? As the days go by, the hope of finding the girl alive turns into a desperate and unrealistic hope.
