Min Skyld - en historie om frigjøring

Abid Raja

Former Minister of Culture Abid Raja has been called loud, posing and dominating. His life has been about the opposite: About hiding the pain and handicap he was born with, to hide from the violence and bullying - and to hide the feelings for the one he loves. This is the brutally honest story about Abid Raja's turbulent social climbing, his long fight for love and the painful liberation from shame, guilt and outsiderness.

Genre: Non-Fiction

Age group: Adult
