Ruffen på nye eventyr

Tor Åge Bringsværd

Come with Ruffen, the sea serpent, on holiday to visit Aunt Nessie in Scotland! When Ruffen gets a letter from Aunt Nessie with an invitation to visit her on holiday, he has no idea how dangerous his travels will be. Ruffen is taken prisoner and put in a cage at the zoo. Will he escape and join Aunt Nessie in the famous Loch Ness?
